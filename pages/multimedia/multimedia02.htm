Audio

Below you can find a very nice audio player created by e-phonic and in the playlist, you can find eight MP3 songs that was collected from the Awakening website. Six of those are especially made for the races, with other words, those are only played if one of those races are being played. The other two are general ambient music, which could be played regardless of race.

On the next page you will find video files that can either be viewed in the current browser, or send you to Youtube where you can also view them.
