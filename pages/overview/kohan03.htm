Unit summary

On this page you can find a list of the all the Faction units that was earlier described and their aspects. The Overview continues on the next page..



Faction list

The Wizard unit
Health: 120 Upkeep: 3 Mana
AV: 8 Type: Magic Assult
DV: 0 Requirements: Library

Spells at Recruit, Regular and Veteran levels:
Shock/Storm Shield: inflict a single 8 point Magical damage on enemies as well as provide resistance against Ranged attacks with +20% for 30 seconds.
Lightning: inflict a single 125 point Magical damage every 6th second.

Spells at Elite level:
Burn/Storm Armor: inflict a single 12 point Magical damage on enemies as well as provide resistance against Ranged attacks with +40% for 30 seconds.
Lightning Bolt: inflict a single 150 point Magical damage every 6th second.

The Paladin unit
Health: 460 Upkeep: 1 Iron 1 Mana
AV: 40 Type: Cavalry Assult
DV: 5 Requirements: Barracks, Shrine

The Paladin unit provides Inspiration: Which provides +2 AV , +1 DV as well as -33% Morale Loss Rate. They also give +20% resistance in Melee as well as Ranged attacks for all units in the company.

The Zealot unit
Health: 160 Upkeep: 3 Mana
AV: 10 Type: Magic Assult
DV: 3 Requirements: Shrine

Spells at Recruit, Regular and Veteran levels:
Wash of Pain: inflicts 25 points in Magical damage every 10th second, as well as punishing the enemy by lowering Melee (-20%), Ranged (-20%) as well as Siege (-20%) resistance for a duration of 20 seconds.
Invigorate: provides an increase in health for the entire company with +30% for a duration of 30 seconds.

Spells at Elite level:
Wave of Torment: inflicts 35 points in Magical damage every 10th second, as well as punishing the enemy by lowering Melee (-40%), Ranged (-20%) as well as Siege (-20%) resistance for a duration of 20 seconds.
Fortify: provides an increase in health for the entire company with +50% for a duration of 30 seconds.

The Zealot unit provides Frenzy: Which increases the Morale by 2 points as well as lowering the Morale Loss Rate with -33% for the entire company.

The Prophet unit
Health: 120 Upkeep: 3 Mana
AV: 8 Type: Single Healing
DV: 3 Requirements: Shrine

Spells at Recruit, Regular and Veteran levels:
Life Leech: inflict 25 points in a single Magical damage.
Dark Restoration: heals a single unit with 150 points every 6th second.

Spells at Elite level:
Life Drain: inflict 36 points in a single Magical damage.
Shadow Restoration: heals a single unit with 200 points every 6th second.

The Dreadlord unit
Health: 580 Upkeep: 1 Iron 2 Mana
AV: 40 Type: Cavalry Assult
DV: 5 Requirements: Barracks, Shrine

The Dreadlord unit provides Bloodlust: Which provides the entire company with +3 AV as well as increasing the Melee and Ranged resistance with +20%. They also provide an ability to negate the Defence Value of the enemy units.
