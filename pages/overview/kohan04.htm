Gameplay

There are alot of elements in this game that you have to keep track of. Some examples are; companies and units to recruit, to organize them in the field of battle and manage them while retreating. Technologies to master and to use correctly, economies to balance and of course those unforeseen events occurring.

When it comes to the units, I will now give a few examples. In the group of images below you can find the first group containing of six Infantry elements, one from each race. You have (from the left); Swordsman, Stonewalker, Berzerker, Anvil, Skeleton and Reaver.

After those we have another group, containing of six Archer elements. You have (from the left); Bowman, Rainbringer, Impaler, Spear, Bone Archer and Fury.
