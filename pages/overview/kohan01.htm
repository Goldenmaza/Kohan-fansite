The Game

Kohan II: Kings of War is a real-time strategy game (RTS). Here you get the chance to control six different races; Human, Haroun, Drauga, Gauri, Undead, and The Shadow. Each are ruled by unique factions; Council, Royalist, Nationalist, Ceyah, and the Fallen.

The Human race supports all factions except the Fallen. The Haroun race only support the Royalist and the Council faction. The Drauga supports the Royalist and the Nationalist factions. The Gauri support the Council and the Nationalist factions. The Undead supports the Ceyah and the Fallen factions. The Shadow only listen to one master and that is the Fallen faction.

Each have a special unit that can be used in the war against the others. The Council faction have the Wizard unit. The Royalist faction have the Paladin unit. The Nationalist faction have the Zealot unit. The Ceyah faction have the Prophet unit. The Fallen faction have the Dreadlord unit.

Even if I say that races only support a special faction, that doesn't mean that they can't be captured or utilized, to some degree, by another faction.
