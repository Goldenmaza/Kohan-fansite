Recruiting

A good company setup means alot in the outcome of a game. As an example; 4x of Archer elements as front, 2x of Infantry elements as flank, 1x or 2x of different Healing support units (a multiple and a single unit healing). You can also go by using 1x of Healing support and 1x support unit that gives either Defensive Tactics or Combat Tactics for the whole company. Incase one of those units doesnt excists, for whatever reason, a combat support unit will also be good. Examples of combat support units are Warden (Gauri), Tracker (Gauri) and Revenant (Undead).

This setup can be used for most races and factions, some units might change. For Shadow it will be alittle different, since they use another type of company setup, with just a single leader that also act as a support unit.

If you play this game you wont be dissapointed, this is one of the best strategy games out there. What are you waiting for? Go, shoo!
