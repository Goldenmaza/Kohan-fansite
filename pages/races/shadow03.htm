Support units

On this page you can find a list of all support units that are being used by the Shadow race. These three units are also the leaders of the company that they are placed on. That is the difference between the Shadow race and the others. With other words they have no traditional support units and a leader. For the Shadow race, they are one and the same. The remaining units can be found on the next page..



Support list

The Banshee unit
Health: 180 Upkeep: 2 Mana
AV: 10 Type: Single Healing
DV: 2 Requirements: Font of Sorrow

Spells at Recruit, Regular and Veteran levels:
Unholy Strength: provides +4 AV for a duration of 30 seconds.
Dark Restoration: heals a single unit with 150 points every 6th second.

Spells at Elite level:
Unholy Power: provides +7 AV for a duration of 30 seconds.
Shadow Restoration: heals a single unit with 200 points every 6th second.

The Banshee unit provides Archer Foe: Which provides every unit in the company with +5 AV against enemy Archer.

The Warlock unit
Health: 240 Upkeep: 3 Mana
AV: 10 Type: Collective Lockdown
DV: 3 R: Font of Sorrow, Tower of Pain

Spells at Recruit, Regular and Veteran levels:
Umbral Shield: provides resistance against Melee attacks with +20% for 30 seconds.
Paralyze/Shadow Shock: paralyze 5 units and inflict 20 points in Magical damage for a duration of 5 respectivly 10 seconds.

Spells at Elite level:
Umbral Armor: provides resistance against Melee as well as Ranged attacks with +20% for 30 seconds.
Paralyze/Shadow Burn: paralyze 5 units and inflict 30 points in Magical damage for a duration of 6 respectivly 10 seconds.

The Warlock unit provides Infantry Foe: Which provides every unit in the company with +5 AV against enemy Infantry.

The Fiend unit
Health: 280 Upkeep: 2 Mana
AV: 20 Type: Summoner
DV: 2 Requirements: Mana Focus

Spells at Recruit, Regular and Veteran levels:
Fear/Umbral Aura: punishes the enemy by decreasing -10% Morale for an enemy company as well as increasing 1+ DV for the entire company over a duration of 30 second.
Shadowspawn: summons 4 Shadelings that attack the enemy units or buildings.

Spells at Elite level:
Terror/Aura of Fear: punishes the enemy by decreasing -20% Morale for an enemy company as well as increasing 2+ DV for the entire company over a duration of 30 second.
Shadowswarm: summons 2 Shadelings as well as 2 Wargs that attack the enemy units or buildings.

The Fiend unit provides Cavalry Foe: Which provides every unit in the company with +5 AV against enemy Cavalry.
