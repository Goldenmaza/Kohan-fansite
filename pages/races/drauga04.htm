Remaining units

On this page you can find a list of all other units that are being used by the Drauga race, the Packbeast unit is special in the case it can not be used as a frontline element, just like with the Ox unit for the Human race. Instead they are automaticly placed as flanks, on companies that have a frontline containing of Settlers.
