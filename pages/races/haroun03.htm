Support units

On this page you can find a list of all support units that are being used by the Haroun race. The remaining units can be found on the next page..



Support list

The Moonwatcher unit
Health: 120 Upkeep: 2 Mana
AV: 8 Type: Collective Healing
DV: 0 Requirements: Moonlit Altar

Spells at Recruit, Regular and Veteran levels:
Holy Shield: provides +5 DV for a duration of 30 seconds.
Revive: heals the entire company with 25 points every 6th second.

Spells at Elite level:
Holy Armor: provides +7 DV for a duration of 30 seconds.
Renew: heals the entire company with 35 points every 6th second.

The Harvester unit
Health: 220 Upkeep: 1 Wood 2 Mana
AV: 20 Type: Collective Lockdown
DV: 3 Requirements: Grove of Dusk

Spells at Recruit, Regular and Veteran levels:
Whirlwind: punishes the enemy by decreasing -2 AV as well as -2 DV from 8 units for a duration of 8 second.
Grasping Thorns: entagles 8 units and inflict 5 points in Physical damage for a duration of 10 seconds.

Spells at Elite level:
Tornado: punishes the enemy by decreasing -3 AV as well as -3 DV from 8 units for a duration of 10 seconds.
Entagling Thorns: entagles 10 units and inflict 6 points in Physical damage for a duration of 10 seconds.

The Pathfinder unit
Health: 220 Upkeep: 1 Wood 1 Mana
AV: 32 22 Type: Archer Assult
DV: 1 Req: Gift of Wood, Grove of Dusk

The Pathfinder unit is much like the Ranger unit, by providing Trailblazing: Terrain Speed conditions are uneffected and adds +3 to the Detection Range. They also give +40% resistance in Siege attacks for all units in the company.
