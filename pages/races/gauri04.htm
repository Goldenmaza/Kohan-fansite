Remaining units

On this page you can find a list of all other units that are being used by the Gauri race, the Baluch unit is special in the case it can not be used as a frontline element, just like with the Packbeast unit for the Drauga race. Instead they are automaticly placed as flanks, on companies that have a frontline containing of Founders.
