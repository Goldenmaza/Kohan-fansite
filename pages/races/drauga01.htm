The Drauga race

The Drauga race lacks in magical elements but have a very strong infantry and cavalry that negate that setback. Their archer elements aren't as strong as the Haroun or even the Human race, but they have other features to help in that regard.

Their settlements have the ability to negate the effects caused by the amount of iron that is needed for their infantry and the cavalry elements, by upgrading the market in a settlement.
