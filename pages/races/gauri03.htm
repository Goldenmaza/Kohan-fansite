Support units

On this page you can find a list of all support units that are being used by the Gauri race. The remaining units can be found on the next page..



Support list

The Healer unit
Health: 160 Upkeep: 2 Mana
AV: 8 Type: Collective Healing
DV: 2 Requirements: Reliquary

Spells at Recruit, Regular and Veteran levels:
Slow: punishes the enemy by decreasing -1 AV from 8 units for a duration of 8 second.
Revive: heals the entire company with 25 points every 6th second. As well as provide the company with +25% Morale.

Spells at Elite level:
Ebb: punishes the enemy by decreasing -2 AV from 8 units for a duration of 8 second.
Renew: heals the entire company with 35 points every 6th second. As well as provide the company with +50% Morale.

The Wrecker unit
Health: 380 Upkeep: 2 Stone 2 Iron
AV: 32 Type: Infantry Assult
DV: 3 Req: Excavation, Garrison

Spells at Recruit, Regular and Veteran levels:
Immobilize: entagles units and make them unable to fight back (Paralyze ) for a duration of 16 seconds.

Spells at Elite level:
Petrify: entagles units and make them unable to fight back (Paralyze ) for a duration of 24 seconds.

The Wrecker unit provides Recon as well as being a Tactician: Which provides +6 Detection Range to the entire company. They also provide +2 AV as well as +25% Entrenchment Rate.

The Warden unit
Health: 420 Upkeep: 1 Iron 1 Mana
AV: 38 Type: Infantry Assult
DV: 4 Requirements: Archive

The Warden unit provides Aura of Immunity: Which provides +2 DV as well as +20% Magic resistance for the entire company. They also provide +20% resistance in both Melee as well as Ranged attacks for all units in the company.
